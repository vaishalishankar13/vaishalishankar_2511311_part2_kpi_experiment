Executive Summary

The onboarding treatment significantly improved paid conversion and user engagement.

Paid conversion increased from 3.19% to 7.04%, representing more than a 120% relative lift.

Statistical testing confirmed the improvement is significant (p-value = 0.00055).

However, support ticket rate increased from 14.8% to 24.8%, suggesting the new experience may create user confusion or operational burden.

Recommendation
Launch Only For Selected Segments

Recommended rollout:
Mobile users
Referral traffic users
North and South regions

while investigating support ticket increases before a full rollout.

Risks
Higher support volume
Small increase in refunds
Long-term retention not measured

Next Steps
Investigate support ticket root causes.
Improve onboarding messaging.
Run a follow-up experiment.
Measure 60-day retention and churn.
Expand rollout if support metrics improve.
